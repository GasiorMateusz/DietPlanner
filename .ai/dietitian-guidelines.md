# Practical Open-Access Guidelines, Calculation Methods, and APIs for Dietitians

A curated, ready-to-use collection of open/free European and global resources for calculating macronutrient needs and building 7-day, individualized meal plans for patients, including those with clinical conditions or intolerances.

Resources are grouped in four key categories. Each entry includes a brief utility description and access method (API, PDF, or HTML). For high-impact resources, direct citations are noted.

---

## 1. Clinical Guidelines & Frameworks  
_Reference standards for clinical nutrition and medical nutrition therapy (MNT)_

- **EFSA — Dietary Reference Values (DRVs) & Food-Based Dietary Guidelines**  
  *Why useful:* Authoritative EU reference for nutrient targets, including %EI ranges, PRI/AI/AR, etc.  
  **Access:** [EFSA DRV pages & PDF summaries](https://www.efsa.europa.eu/en/topics/topic/nutrition)
  
- **ESPEN (European Society for Clinical Nutrition and Metabolism)**  
  *Why useful:* Clinical nutrition guidelines for energy/protein prescription, adult & pediatric MNT, inpatient and community care.  
  **Access:** [ESPEN website/Guidelines (PDFs)](https://www.espen.org/guidelines-home/espen-guidelines)  

- **NICE (UK) — Nutrition support in adults**  
  *Why useful:* Practical guidance for malnutrition assessment, nutrition support, clinical targets.  
  **Access:** [NICE guidance pages (includes syndication/API)](https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/cg32)  

- **KDOQI/Kidney Foundation — Nutrition in CKD (2020 update)**  
  *Why useful:* Prescribing energy and protein for chronic kidney disease; essential for renal MNT.  
  **Access:** [Kidney Foundation/Guidelines (PDFs)](https://www.kidney.org/professionals/guidelines)  

- **EAACI — Food Allergy Guidelines**  
  *Why useful:* Evidence-based advice for intolerance/allergy management in food planning.  
  **Access:** [EAACI guidelines & PDFs](https://www.eaaci.org/guidelines)  

- **Diabetes Nutrition Therapy Consensus (ADA/EASD/National)**  
  *Why useful:* Carb counting, macro targets, insulin-to-carb ratios, applied diabetes nutrition.  
  **Access:** [Diabetes Care consensus reports & EASD statements](https://diabetesjournals.org/care)

## 2. Key Scientific Methodology Sources  
_Equations and standards for energy/macro calculations_

- **FAO/WHO/UNU — Human Energy Requirements (2001/2004)**  
  *Why useful:* Canonical reference for estimating energy needs (BMR, PAL, illness factors).  
  **Access:** [FAO full report](https://www.fao.org/publications/card/en/c/3e7f526e-2081-5ef4-965a-4fb151797f45)  

- **Predictive Equations:**  
  *Why useful:* Gold-standard BMR predictive equations  
    - *Schofield* (FAO/WHO 1985)  
    - *Harris-Benedict*  
    - *Mifflin-St Jeor* (especially for adults)  
  **Access:** [NutriWeb review of equations](https://www.nutriweb.org.my/article.php?sid=82)

- **ESPEN — Illness Stress Factors & Clinical Protein Needs**  
  *Why useful:* Adjusts requirements based on disease state/inflammation.  
  **Access:** [ESPEN guidelines](https://www.espen.org/guidelines-home/espen-guidelines)  

- **SACN (UK) — Carbohydrates & Health (2015)**  
  *Why useful:* Detailed evidence and policy for carbohydrate/fibre/sugar goals, key for cardiometabolic planning.  
  **Access:** [SACN Report (PDF)](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/sacn-carbohydrates-and-health-report)

## 3. Professional Tools, Calculators & Templates  
_Operational guidance for 7-day meal planning_

- **ESPEN/National Dietetic Templates**  
  *Why useful:* Downloadable MNT/meal plan templates, calculation workflows.  
  **Access:** [ESPEN guideline appendices & national associations](https://www.espen.org/guidelines-home/espen-guidelines)  

- **NICE Evidence & Calculation Examples**  
  *Why useful:* Evidence and worked calculation appendices for 7-day planning.  
  **Access:** [NICE guidance](https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/cg32/evidence)  

- **WHO/FAO Calculation Tables + PAL Guidance**  
  *Why useful:* Standard PAL multipliers for energy calculation.  
  **Access:** [FAO tables](https://www.fao.org/3/y5686e/y5686e07.htm)  

- **EFSA/FAO Index of Food-Based Dietary Guidelines (FBDGs)**  
  *Why useful:* For menu translation of macro targets by cultural/food context.  
  **Access:** [FBDGs regional index](https://www.fao.org/nutrition/education/food-dietary-guidelines/en/)

## 4. Open Literature & Programmatic Access  
_Essential APIs and repositories for automation or literature retrieval_

- **Europe PMC — RESTful Articles API**  
  *Why useful:* Programmatic search/download of biomedical publications, consensus, guidelines.  
  **Access:** [Europe PMC API docs](https://europepmc.org/RestfulWebService)  
  *API supports JSON/XML, DOI/PMID queries, filters, paging.*  

- **NCBI PubMed — E-utilities (Entrez API)**  
  *Why useful:* Automated search/retrieval of biomedical articles, including EFSA, WHO, and guideline papers.  
  **Access:** [NCBI E-utilities](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK25501/)  

- **NICE Guidance Syndication/API**  
  *Why useful:* Programmatic access to NICE guideline texts/recommendations.  
  **Access:** [NICE API](https://developer.nice.org.uk/)  

- **Europe/UK Govt Repositories**  
  *Why useful:* Download citable guideline PDFs (SACN, national reports).  
  **Access:** [gov.uk government publications](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications)
